QUIET QUESTIONS

What is K-beauty brightening, really?

Not whitening. Not bleaching. Brightening is the slow art of layering light back into skin — through hydration, barrier repair, and pigment balance. It takes rituals, not injections.

How often should I come?

Most rituals settle in over three visits across six to eight weeks. Some bloom faster. We'll pace it together.

Is this safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. Every ritual begins with a patch consultation. Our protocols lean toward calming and barrier-building before anything else.

Do you carry your own products?

A small curated line is in bloom — available only after your first ritual, to ensure the right match.
